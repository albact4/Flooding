Forecast of Flash Flooding

Visualize and prepare for the impacts of heavy rains and flash flooding in urban areas near the Charleston-Kanawha River, West Virginia.

Charleston, WV, and its surroundings face a unique challenge due to the hilly terrain, limited available land for large settlements, and a high number of streams that can rapidly become fast-flowing during heavy storms. These factors make flash flooding a serious concern for the community.
We serves as an invaluable resource for residents, businesses, and commuters, providing critical information about flash flooding risks in the region.
